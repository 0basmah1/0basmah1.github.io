Waqf Protection System

Islamic endowments face serious challenges with document loss and mismanagement, Important Waqf documents often go missing, funds get misused.

This project presents a secure digital watermarking system designed to protect sensitive Awqaf documents from tampering, unauthorized access, and misuse.
The system embeds a hidden watermark chosen by the waqf trustee using Discrete Wavelet Transform (DWT) combined with either the Least
Significant Bit (LSB) technique or the additive technique, in addition to a font change technique for text-based protection.
These methods ensure document integrity, enable authenticity verification, and allow quick detection of any modification while preserving the original document quality.
